The purpose of Flux is to give real democratic potential to every person.

Flux is an unregistered political party designed to create an accessible, productive, and above all, fair, democracy.

Party membership is free, easy, and open to all Australian voters. We are required to have 500 members before we can register.

Why do we need Flux?

Our current system doesn't work well enough; politics gets in the way of policy. Flux is an incremental upgrade to democracy designed to redistribute political power, maximise participation, remove bad policy, and empower voters.

The Australian Parliament is based on exclusion and exclusivity and does not equip us with the right tools to solve modern problems to the best of our ability; Flux guarentees the best solutions will never be excluded. In order to solve problems that have never been solved before we need to try things that have never been tried before. Flux embodies this value, and facilitates it.

We believe the best way forward is not to focus on individual solutions but to focus on how we arrive at those solutions, and how we maintain them over time.

What is Flux

Flux, as a party, is a layer for the redistribution of political power. When a Flux candidate is elected they become a gateway for voters to directly influence parliament. Unlike other representatives, Flux candidates are not autonomous, and their votes in parliament are determined wholly by Flux participants.

Flux, as a system, is a way to cooperate with fellow voters and distribute power in order to increase the amount of good legislation passed, and make passing bad legislation very expensive and difficult. The focus of Flux is not to be absolutely proportional, or give all voters an equal say on everything. Rather, Flux aims to solve particular problems by enabling those who are particularly well suited to solve that problem.

How does Flux work?

Flux has many features that are build on fundamentals of economics and philosophy. By providing an open system (in which any voter can participate) we encourage healthy development of policy, the suggestion of new conjecture and ideas, and a culture of criticism necessary for a healthy democracy.

Flux is designed to offer something to everyone. It does this by enabling specialization and so provides a platform for issue based politics, something that is not possible in the generalist Westminster system. Furthermore, it allows projects of any length to be undertaken without being interrupted by the changing political landscape. Long term policy can be worked towards by dedicated invidivuals even if the timeframe is well beyond a term. Short term innovative policy, or updates to past policy can be undertaken with similar ease as needed, without requiring prior political interest. Provided the policy is of high quality, Flux is designed to make this as simple and painless as possible.

Neutrality and openess to alternate ideas are crucial to Flux operating as a platform that everyone can use, in the same way the Westminster system doesn't dictate which policies are acceptable. Flux has no policies of its own in order to protect the integrity of this platform.

Who is behind Flux?

We are a group of motivated Australian's passionate about democracy, technology, sovling real problems, and permissionless innovation. If you'd like to meet us, you can join our Slack team.

We do not believe the current system of governance is adequate for today's world, and we are determined to do something about it. We feel disenfranchised and do not identify with major political parties like the ALP, LNP, or Greens. We are particularly dissuaded by the way that every major political party has lost their integrity and become self-serving, instead of representing the interests of society.

We feel that in order to raise living standards, solve global problems, provide employment, lower crime, and solve all other social evils, we must embrace the best solution and best policy, even if we disagree with it. We must have policy backed by the best explanations, not the most voices.

Features of Flux

Flux is a system built on solid economic foundations, and offers a wide range of features that are not offered by canonical democracies.

Empower Specialists
Politicians are middle men between specialists and policy, and by cutting them out of the picture we enable specialists to create the best policy they can, unmolested by external political interests.
Built in Progress Through Trade
Trading resources is one of the main mechanics behind both the redistribution of wealth and growing prosperity around the globe. Flux introduces this mechanic to the political arena, enabling innovators and democratic entrupenurs to solve problems with minimal hinderance.
Fully Transparent
Flux doesn't have absolute control over the voting system, which is run on a distributed public network and is able to be verified by everyone, everywhere, constantly, and consistently.
Stops Backroom Deals (the right way)
By embracing specialization Flux does away with the "one-person-for-all-issues" model, elimintating the potential and motivation for backroom deals.
Favours High Quality Legislation
Specialists naturally produce better quality legislation than generalists (like politicians), and by introducing opportunity cost and comparitive advantage for legislators we help only the best legislation rise to the surface.
Empower Voters
Flux enables regular Australians to become fully involved in the political process, and to focus on the issues they care most about.
Encourages Criticism and Debate
The ecosystem within Flux encourages a culture of criticism around new policy before it's enacted, and demands that policy options can withstand that criticism.
Ends Corruption
Under Flux, lobbiests wouldn't need to convince politicians, they would need to convince the people. In this way special interests become directly accountable to the community they affect, and can't elbow their way through.
Facilitates Conjecture
Flux is like an arena for ideas. By ensuring the best ideas are heard we create an open culture where conjecture is shared and innovation is encouraged and supported.
